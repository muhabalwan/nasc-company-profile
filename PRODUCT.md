# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Primary users are mixed stakeholder groups across the oil and gas value chain, with the homepage expected to speak first to operators, procurement contacts, and potential partners assessing execution reliability in Syria. They visit in a high-risk, trust-sensitive context and need quick proof of competence, safety discipline, and responsiveness.

## Product Purpose
This website positions NASC for Power Solutions as a dependable field execution partner for pipelines, surface facilities, sludge treatment, and water injection services. Success means qualified inbound contact through direct channels (email, phone, form) from decision-ready prospects.

## Brand Personality
Reliable, human, premium.

Tone should project operational confidence and discipline while staying clear, respectful, and approachable in both English and Arabic. The emotional target is trust through clarity, not hype.

## Anti-references
- Generic corporate template aesthetics that feel interchangeable and low-commitment.
- Empty marketing language without operational specificity.
- Visual choices that prioritize decoration over credibility and readability.

## Design Principles
- Evidence over claims: show capability through specific service language and credible detail.
- Clarity under pressure: decision makers should find scope, trust signals, and contact paths quickly.
- Bilingual parity: Arabic and English experiences should feel equally intentional, not translated afterthoughts.
- Premium restraint: visual quality should feel deliberate and elevated without flashy effects.
- Contact immediacy: every key section should reduce friction toward direct outreach.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Current baseline is basic readability and usability while preserving bilingual clarity.

Implementation priorities now:
- Maintain clear contrast and legible text in both language modes.
- Keep link and form states recognizable for keyboard and touch users.
- Avoid motion that distracts from content comprehension.

Target can be raised to WCAG AA in a future hardening pass.
